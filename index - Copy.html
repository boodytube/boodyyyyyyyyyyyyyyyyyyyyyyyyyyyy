Children Museum

At the Children's Museum, kids can embark on a magical journey back in time,
exploring the lives of their grandparents and experiencing the beauty of ancient traditions.
Through interactive exhibits and hands-on activities, they get a glimpse of how people lived
in the past — from the houses they built to the clothes they wore and the tools they used.
It’s a wonderful adventure that brings history to life and connects generations in the most
captivating way!
